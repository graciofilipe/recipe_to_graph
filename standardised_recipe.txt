```
Ingredients:

Vegetable Preparation:
* 1 large onion, finely chopped
* 2 carrots, peeled and diced
* 2 celery stalks, diced
* 3 cloves garlic, minced

Spices:
* 1 tsp ground turmeric
* 1 tsp ground cumin
* 1 tsp ground ginger
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Liquids:
* 1 tbsp olive oil
* 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
* 6 cups vegetable broth (low sodium)
* Juice of 1 lemon

Lentils and Herbs:
* 1 (15-ounce) can lentils, rinsed and drained
* 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
* 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
* 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint (optional)

Steps:

Independent Preparation:
1.  Prepare Vegetables: Finely chop the onion, dice the carrots and celery, and mince the garlic.
2.  Rinse and drain the canned lentils.
3.  Chop the cilantro, parsley, and mint (if using).

Soup Cooking:
1.  Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
2.  Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery to the pot and saut√© for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened.
3.  Add the minced garlic, turmeric, cumin, ginger, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper to the pot and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, until fragrant.
4.  Stir in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a simmer.
5.  Add the rinsed and drained lentils to the pot.
6.  Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
7.  Stir in the chopped cilantro, parsley, and mint (if using).
8.  Add the lemon juice.

```
